About

  • Android and iOS app for eighth largest chess website in the world (according to Alexa), chessgames.com.
  • Allows anyone to play chess against anyone in the world.
  • Written entirely from scratch using C++.
  • Completely free and open source.
  • Available in English, Spanish, Mandarin and Russian.
  • Will be released on Android and iOS app stores in April.
  • Written from September 29, 2014 - Present.
